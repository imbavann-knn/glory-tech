Our Solutions

End-to-End Cooling
Delivery

From initial thermal audit to long-term maintenance contracts — one partner for the full lifecycle of your data centre cooling infrastructure.

01

Thermal Audit & Consultancy

We begin with a comprehensive site assessment — mapping existing heat loads, identifying hot spots, reviewing airflow patterns, and benchmarking current PUE. Our engineers deliver a detailed findings report with prioritised recommendations.

02

Solution Design & Engineering

Our design team develops a tailored cooling architecture — selecting the right mix of products, specifying capacity and redundancy levels, and producing full engineering drawings. Every design is validated against ASHRAE standards.

03

Supply & Procurement

As authorised distributors and system integrators, we source precision cooling equipment from leading global manufacturers, managing logistics, import, and delivery to your site.

04

Installation & Commissioning

Certified installation teams execute the deployment with minimal disruption to live operations. Full commissioning includes functional testing, performance verification, and handover documentation.

05

Maintenance & Support Contracts

Flexible service agreements covering preventive maintenance schedules, emergency response SLAs, spare parts management, and remote monitoring — keeping your cooling infrastructure operating at specification year-round.
