Five Advantages of Liquid Cooling

Energy Efficiency

Industry-Leading PUE

Backplane-type liquid cooling achieves PUE <1.30. Cold-plate solutions deliver PUE <1.15 — far below the industry average of 1.5+.

High Refrigeration Capacity

600W to 1500W Per Chip

Handles 15–60kW per cabinet and supports chips up to 1500W — scaling with the most demanding AI accelerators.

Easy to Modify

Non-Stop Retrofit Path

Existing air-cooled data centres can be retrofitted to liquid cooling without shutdown. Achieve up to 60% energy savings while preserving existing infrastructure investment.

Safe & Stable

Non-Toxic, Non-Flammable Coolant

Proprietary dielectric fluid is electrically insulating, non-toxic and non-flammable. Multi-level redundancy across CDUs, pumps, and chillers ensures continuous uptime.

Easy to Maintain

Zero Scheduled Fluid Maintenance

Closed-loop system requires no fluid replacement or degassing under normal operation. Service intervals are driven by component condition, not coolant degradation.

Retrofit Solution

From Air Cooling to Liquid Cooling

A proven five-step process to upgrade existing air-cooled data centres to backplane-type two-phase liquid cooling — without any service interruption.

1
Site Assessment
Evaluate existing thermal load, rack layout, and power capacity
2
Install New CDU
Deploy cooling distribution units alongside active infrastructure
3
Add Integrated Chiller
Commission outdoor dry coolers or chiller units for warm-climate operation
4
Replace Backplane Cooling
Swap air-cooled rear-door panels for liquid-cooled backplate heat exchangers, rack by rack
5
System Switchover
Migrate thermal load to liquid loop and decommission legacy air conditioning
